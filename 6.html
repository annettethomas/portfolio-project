PERSONA

Description

For my introduction to UX class, I created a "persona" named Jolene that related to the topic I had for a project. My persona was a shop-a-holic that wanted to unwind and shop for cute clothes that she had found online.

Details

Process

My persona wants to shop online, but is worried about the amount of time it takes to have a product delivered to you depending on where the product is originally located. She has also heard terrifying stories of people's online experiences with scammers.
Jolene wants to shop online and know she is safe doing so.
