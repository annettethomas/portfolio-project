S2S

Description

I currently work at the Multicultural Center of my University as a web designer! There, I design pages for an organization called Student2Student which is a community that encourages, empowers, and inspires high school students about the importance of their education and to help them transtion to college if they are interested.

Details

Process

I used figma to create my wireframe and prototype design of the website. Each with a mobile and desktop version to show how the page will respond to the specific screen sizes. Once this step was completed, I used bootstrap and flex grids to code the website.
